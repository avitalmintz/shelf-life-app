# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026

Shelf Life: Screenshot Saver helps you save, organize, and revisit screenshots.

## Information You Provide

Shelf Life stores screenshots, titles, notes, categories, links, reminder dates, and app settings that you add or create in the app.

## Screenshot Analysis

When AI categorization or source-link lookup is enabled, screenshots and related text may be sent to our backend service so the app can suggest a title, category, notes, and relevant source links. The backend may use third-party AI and search providers to process these requests.

## Local Storage

Your saved screenshots and related app data are stored on your device. Shelf Life does not provide account sync at this time.

## Notifications

If you set reminders, Shelf Life may schedule local notifications on your device. You can manage notification permissions in iOS Settings.

## Affiliate Links

Shelf Life may identify or display links related to saved screenshots. Some links may be affiliate links, which means we may earn a commission if you make a purchase through them, at no additional cost to you.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your saved screenshots with other users. Data may be processed by service providers only as needed to provide app functionality, such as AI categorization, source lookup, hosting, and diagnostics.

## Third-Party Links

Shelf Life may show links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites.

## Children

Shelf Life is not directed to children under 13.
